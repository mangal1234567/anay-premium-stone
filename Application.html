Sandstone Applications for Paving, Landscaping, Cladding & Commercial Projects

ANAY Premium Stone is a quarry owner, sandstone manufacturer, processor, supplier, wholesaler, exporter and bulk supplier of Dholpur Beige Sandstone, Dholpur Red Sandstone and Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone for paving, landscaping, flooring, wall cladding, commercial developments, public infrastructure and large-scale construction projects worldwide.

Exterior Wall Cladding

Sandstone wall cladding is widely used for residential buildings, commercial developments, hospitality projects, institutional buildings and public infrastructure. Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red and Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone provide durability, weather resistance and long-term visual appeal.

Landscape & Paving Systems

ANAY Premium Stone supplies sandstone paving, cobbles, kerbstones and landscape products for gardens, walkways, plazas, resorts, public spaces, commercial developments and infrastructure projects. Natural sandstone offers durability, slip resistance and long-term performance.

Flooring, Steps & Cut-To-Size Products

Sandstone slabs, tiles, steps and cut-to-size products are widely used for residential, commercial and public projects. Custom dimensions and finishes are available to meet project-specific requirements.

Commercial Buildings & Infrastructure Projects

Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red and Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone are supplied for commercial developments, educational campuses, hospitality projects, retail complexes, public infrastructure and large-scale construction projects worldwide.
