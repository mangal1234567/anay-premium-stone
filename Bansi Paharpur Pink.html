Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone Manufacturer, Supplier & Exporter – Rajasthan India

Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone is a fine-grained natural stone extracted from the Bansi Paharpur region of Rajasthan, India. Known for its soft blush tone, durability and uniform structure, it is widely used in temples, villas, resorts, facades, landscaping, wall cladding, paving, heritage restoration and commercial construction projects.

ANAY Premium Stone manufactures, supplies and exports Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone in slabs, tiles, paving stones, wall cladding and custom cut-to-size products for temples, villas, resorts, landscapers, contractors and heritage projects worldwide.

Quarry Source

The stone is quarried in the Bansi Paharpur belt of Rajasthan, a geological region known for producing dense and durable architectural sandstone widely used in temples, villas, resorts, landscaping projects, heritage restoration and monumental architecture.

Why Buyers Choose Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone

Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone is widely selected for temples, luxury residences, heritage-inspired architecture, carved facades, and landscape features because of its distinctive natural color and excellent workability.

The stone is available in slabs, tiles, wall cladding, paving stones, steps, risers and custom carved elements for architectural and landscape applications.

Fine grain structure allows CNC machining and hand carving for detailed architectural elements.
