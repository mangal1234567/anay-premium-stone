Quarry Owner, Manufacturer, Supplier & Exporter of Rajasthan Sandstone

Located in Sarmathura, Dholpur, Rajasthan, ANAY Premium Stone operates with direct access to sandstone resources and integrated processing capabilities. Our focus is on delivering consistent quality, dependable supply and export-ready sandstone products for domestic and international markets.

ANAY Premium Stone is a quarry owner, sandstone manufacturer, processor, supplier, wholesaler, exporter and bulk supplier based in Sarmathura, Dholpur, Rajasthan, India. We specialize in Dholpur Beige Sandstone, Dholpur Red Sandstone and Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone for importers, distributors, wholesalers, contractors, landscapers, builders, developers, retailers and commercial projects worldwide.

Our product range includes sandstone blocks, slabs, tiles, paving stones, cobbles, kerbstones, wall cladding, steps and custom cut-to-size products. We supply bulk quantities to importers, distributors, wholesalers, contractors, landscapers, builders, developers and project suppliers across India, USA, UK, UAE, Europe and Australia.

Integrated Manufacturing, Processing & Global Supply

Our manufacturing infrastructure includes calibrated gang saw machines, precision cutting lines, and finishing systems designed for consistent production output.

Production workflows follow strict export standards including dimensional tolerance checks, shade consistency evaluation, and secure palletized container packaging for international shipments.
