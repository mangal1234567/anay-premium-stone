Quarry Owner, Sandstone Manufacturer, Supplier & Exporter in India

ANAY Premium Stone is a quarry owner, sandstone manufacturer, processor, supplier, wholesaler, exporter and bulk supplier based in Dholpur, Rajasthan, India. We supply Dholpur Beige Sandstone, Dholpur Red Sandstone and Bansi Paharpur Pink Sandstone in blocks, slabs, tiles, paving stones, cobbles, kerbstones, wall cladding and custom cut sizes for importers, distributors, contractors, wholesalers and commercial projects worldwide.

Quarry Ownership & Raw Material Control

Direct quarry ownership and raw material control ensure consistent sandstone availability, quality uniformity and dependable supply for domestic and international bulk orders. Every sandstone block is inspected before entering the manufacturing process.

Sandstone Manufacturing Unit & Gang Saw Processing

Our processing facility manufactures sandstone slabs, tiles, paving stones, cobbles, kerbstones, wall cladding, steps and custom cut-to-size products for importers, distributors, wholesalers, contractors and project buyers worldwide.

Quality Control & Bulk Supply Management

Every production batch undergoes dimensional verification, surface inspection and quality checks before dispatch. Structured production planning supports consistent supply for wholesalers, distributors, contractors and large-scale projects.

Global Supply & Export Operations

ANAY Premium Stone supplies sandstone across India and international markets including USA, UK, UAE, Europe and Australia. Export packaging, container loading and shipment planning are managed to support reliable bulk supply and long-term buyer relationships.
